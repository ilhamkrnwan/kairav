## Overview

**Jurutani** is a digital agriculture platform designed to accelerate the modernization and sustainability of farming in Indonesia. It delivers a **comprehensive suite of services** that connects farmers with real-time agricultural data such as weather and market prices, expert consultation, community forums, educational resources, and marketplace access — all in one integrated ecosystem. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Jurutani’s core mission is to bridge the gap between traditional farming practices and digital innovation, enabling farmers of all experience levels to make informed decisions, collaborate with peers and professionals, and grow their productivity sustainably. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

The platform includes features such as **expert chats and consultations**, **agricultural courses and tutorials**, **interactive community discussion rooms**, and a **marketplace for selling products directly**, empowering farmers to improve efficiency and gain better access to opportunities. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Built with **Nuxt.js, Capacitor, Tailwind CSS, Google Gemini AI integrations, and Supabase**, the project emphasizes **mobile responsiveness** and **performance**, making it accessible across devices for farmers in the field. The product is intended to be a powerful digital companion throughout the agricultural lifecycle.
