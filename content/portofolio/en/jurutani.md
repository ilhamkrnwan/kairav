# Jurutani — All-in-One Digital Agriculture Platform

The all-in-one digital agriculture platform empowering Indonesian farmers with real-time data, AI-powered insights, expert consultation, and integrated marketplace access.

![Jurutani Preview](/portofolio/jurutani/preview.avif)

## Problem

Indonesian farmers face unpredictable weather, fluctuating market prices, limited access to agricultural experts, and outdated farming techniques. Existing digital solutions are often too complicated or fail to address the daily needs of farmers in the field.

## Solution

Jurutani was built as a mobile-first platform that unifies all agricultural needs in a single application:

- **Real-Time Intelligence** — Live weather updates, current commodity prices, market trends, and automated crop calendars
- **AI-Powered Consultation** — Google Gemini AI integration for instant farming advice and crop disease diagnosis from photos
- **Expert Network** — Direct chat with agricultural officers and scheduled Q&A sessions with experts
- **Community Forum** — Interactive discussion platform for farmers to share experiences and knowledge
- **Educational Library** — Step-by-step farming tutorials, best practices, and seasonal guides
- **Integrated Marketplace** — Direct farm-to-buyer selling platform, reducing dependence on middlemen
- **Mobile-First Design** — Offline capabilities for areas with limited connectivity

## My Role

As **Fullstack Web & Android Developer**, I was responsible for:
- Architecting and developing the entire frontend using Nuxt.js and Capacitor for mobile deployment
- Integrating Google Gemini AI for smart consultation and crop diagnosis features
- Designing and implementing the database using Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- Developing REST APIs for real-time communication between web and mobile platforms
- Implementing responsive, mobile-first UI/UX design

## Tech Stack

| Layer | Technology |
|-------|-----------|
| Frontend | Nuxt.js, Vue 3, Tailwind CSS |
| Mobile | Capacitor (Android & iOS) |
| AI | Google Gemini AI API |
| Backend | Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth, Storage, Realtime) |
| Deployment | Vercel (Web), Google Play Store (Mobile) |

## Impact and Results

- Comprehensive mobile-first platform putting modern agricultural technology in farmers' hands
- Enables data-driven decisions to improve agricultural productivity and profitability
- Reduces middlemen dependency through direct marketplace access
- Provides 24/7 AI consultation access in areas with limited expert availability
