# Building a Modern Web Portfolio

In this fast-paced digital era, having an interactive and _eye-catching_ portfolio is a must for developers and designers alike.

In this article, I want to share a glimpse of my journey building a portfolio using **Nuxt 4** and **Nuxt UI**.

## Why Nuxt 4?

Nuxt 4 offers outstanding performance with _Vue 3_ as its foundation, alongside _Server-Side Rendering_ (SSR) capabilities that provide excellent SEO optimization.

- **Fast:** Thanks to the Nitro server architecture.
- **Developer Experience:** _Auto-imports_ configuration makes development highly intuitive.
- **Flexible:** Can easily be integrated with a vast ecosystem of modules.

> "A great framework gets out of your way and lets you focus on building exactly what you imagine."

## The Elegance of Nuxt UI

Design isn't just about what you see, but how the user experiences it. Choosing _Nuxt UI_ gave me a consistent _Tailwind CSS_ structure paired with a _modern_ theme aesthetic. Specifically:

1. Ease of radically changing themes via CSS Variables (such as _Amber_).
2. Elegant built-in _dark mode_.
3. A suite of responsive, eye-pleasing components.
