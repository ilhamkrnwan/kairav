- Payments, notifications, or reports are still checked manually
- The system is not connected to WhatsApp, email, or storage
- Business wants to use AI but does not yet know the practical workflow
- Data needs to sync with external APIs

I help create secure and structured integrations that follow business workflows, including webhooks, callbacks, payload validation, and logging.
