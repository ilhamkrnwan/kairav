## Service Hero

SEO / GEO Optimization helps websites become easier to find, understand, and cite by search engines and AI-powered generative engines.

## Common Problems

- Pages are online but do not have strong SEO structure
- Copywriting is too generic and does not answer client intent
- Internal links do not guide users to key services
- Content is hard for AI search and generative engines to understand

## Offered Solution

I improve heading structure, metadata, canonical tags, internal links, copywriting, and explicit statements so pages become stronger for search and AI citation.

## Output You Get

- SEO audit
- GEO content improvement
- Metadata optimization
- Clean heading structure
- Internal linking
- Service-oriented copywriting
- Follow-up content recommendations

## Best For

Portfolio websites, company profiles, landing pages, blogs, service pages, and businesses that want clearer positioning in search engines and AI search.

## Example Features

Meta title, description, canonical, Open Graph, FAQ content, internal links, service copy, and content blocks that are easy for AI to cite.

## Work Process

1. Audit pages and content structure
2. Map keywords, intent, and entities
3. Improve metadata, headings, and internal links
4. Rewrite copy for SEO/GEO
5. Recommend next content priorities

## Possible Technologies

Tools and approach depend on the website platform. This may include Nuxt, Nuxt Content, WordPress, schema markup, sitemap, metadata, analytics, and AI-assisted content analysis.
