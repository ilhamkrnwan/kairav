## Service Hero

A powerful combination of an ultra-fast landing page, persuasive copywriting, and optimized ad setups (Meta/Google Ads) to drive quality traffic that converts into active buyers or leads.

## Common Problems

- Running ads but getting zero leads or sales (burning budget)
- Visitors leave the landing page instantly without taking action
- Struggling with technical setups like Meta Pixel, Custom Conversions, or GA4/GTM
- Landing pages are slow, clunky, or lack convincing elements

## Offered Solution

I help construct clean landing pages focused on conversion, integrate technical tracking setups, and design cost-effective ad campaigns to achieve a higher return on investment.

## Output You Get

- High-performance, responsive landing page
- Persuasive, AIDA-structured copywriting
- Complete Meta Pixel & Google Tag Manager configuration
- Campaign setup (audience targeting, ad sets, ad copy creation)
- Basic ad performance dashboard

## Best For

SMEs, online retailers, local services, product launches, and startups looking to get fast, measurable results through paid marketing.

## Example Features

Direct WhatsApp/Email lead forms, database integrations, analytics, multi-pixel support, speed optimization, and benefit-driven structures.

## Work Process

1. Align on product value, target audience, and ad budget
2. Write copywriting and draft landing page layout
3. Develop landing page and integrate tracking pixels
4. Setup ad campaigns (targeting, bids, assets, and copywriting)
5. Final review, launch, and initial performance tracking

## Possible Technologies

For fast landing pages: Nuxt/Vue, Tailwind CSS, Vercel, Supabase (for database/leads), or WordPress/Elementor if content editing is preferred. Ad campaigns are set up on Meta Ads Manager and Google Ads, with tracking handled via Google Tag Manager and GA4.
